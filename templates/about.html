About Blurrinator

Local video privacy redaction for projects where source footage should stay on the workstation.

The project name is a nod to Heinz Doofenshmirtz from Phineas and Ferb: a small, very specific machine built to do one job.

What It Does

Blurrinator is a local Django application for uploading video, detecting faces, reviewing detections manually, and exporting redacted copies. Original files, analysis artifacts, allowlist images, and exports stay on the machine running the app.

The current pipeline focuses on face privacy: YuNet detects faces, SFace creates embeddings for allowlist matching, and manual draft review lets an operator adjust decisions before export.
